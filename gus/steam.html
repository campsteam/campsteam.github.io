Camp STEAM is a Dalton summer camp for students going into the 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th grade. Steam focuses on science, technology, engineering, arts and mathematics. Students can participate in classes about coding, cooking, robotics, projectiles, and engineering with not much experience. By the end of the week, campers had created websites, robots, and even a working hovercraft.
