Welcome!

Camp S.T.E.A.M (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics) is a program run by the Dalton School to teach kids in grades 4-8 more about modern day technology. They're are many diffrent classes to chose from like Intro to HTML, where you can design your very own website, Hack Your Stuff, where you get to break factory made stuff and see how it works, and that is just to name two. There is truly a class for everyone at Steam, just look at these happy campers.
