About

Eucalyptus is a program designed to combine a normal search engine, and a reverse image search to make it easier to connect everything together, and to have a simpiler way to site sources. It simply "reads" the tags of your image, and finds a word that could be used to search it, giving you a general, but more accurate surfing experience, when compared to sites like tin-eye. Enjoy browsing!





(not)Stolen resources

The Google Search Engine was borrowed for a data base, Bootstrap, jquery, and Bootswatch were all used to make interactions possible
